IntellInspect: Revolutionizing Online Assessments with Gaze Tracking Technology

Executive Summary:

IntellInspect is an innovative solution designed to address the growing concern of academic dishonesty in online interviews and student coursework submissions. Leveraging advanced AI technologies, our project introduces a comprehensive approach to ensure the authenticity and integrity of students' work.

Objective:

  • To create a secure and reliable platform for online interviews and coursework submissions.
  • To minimize cheating by implementing AI-generated questions, video responses, and gaze tracking technology.
  • To provide a seamless user experience with an easy-to-use interface.

Key Features:

  1. AI-Generated Questions:
    • Utilizing Chat GPT, we generate dynamic and challenging questions for students to explain their work.
    • Questions are presented one by one, ensuring a focused response and reducing the likelihood of cheating.
  2. Video Responses:
    • Students respond to questions through video submissions, adding an extra layer of authenticity.
    • Arbitrary time limits, determined by question difficulty, discourage the use of external aids.
  3. Gaze Tracking Technology:
    • Integration of an ML model for eye tracking to monitor pupil position during video responses.
    • Analysis of relative pupil position helps identify suspicious behavior, such as looking away or frequent distractions.
  4. Sentiment Analysis:
    • Employing sentiment analysis to gauge the emotional state of the person being interviewed.
    • Provides additional insights into the student's engagement and sincerity.
  5. Web-Based Interface:
    • User-friendly website allowing seamless interaction with the system.
    • Input an essay, receive AI-generated questions, and record video responses within the platform.
  6. Secure Authentication:
    • Robust user authentication with salting and hashing for enhanced security.
    • Protects user accounts from unauthorized access and ensures data confidentiality.

Benefits:

  • Academic Integrity: Ensures that students stand behind their work, fostering a culture of academic honesty.
  • Efficient Evaluation: Simplifies the assessment process for educators with automated analysis and insights.
  • User-Friendly Interface: The intuitive website design facilitates easy navigation for both students and educators.
  • Enhanced Security: Advanced authentication mechanisms safeguard user accounts and sensitive data.

Recommendations:

  • Collaborate with educational institutions to integrate IntellInspect into their online assessment processes.
  • Continuous improvement and updates to the AI models to adapt to evolving cheating techniques.
  • Provide training and support to educators for effective utilization of the platform.

Conclusion:

IntellInspect represents a breakthrough in the fight against academic dishonesty, offering a comprehensive solution that goes beyond conventional assessment methods. By combining AI-generated questions, video responses, gaze tracking, and sentiment analysis, we provide a robust platform that promotes academic integrity in online environments. IntellInspect is poised to revolutionize the way online assessments are conducted, ensuring a fair and secure academic ecosystem.
